Our Approach

A systems-level view of aging

Aging is not a single-gene problem. We address it as a complex systems biology challenge — an integrated platform designed to unravel its intricate networks with the precision modern medicine demands.

01
Precise Biological Control

Pioneering optogenetics to achieve dynamic, reversible control of cellular targets — generating high-fidelity datasets no conventional tool can produce.

02
Expansive Chemical Space

Diverse small-molecule chemistry libraries designed to explore previously inaccessible regions of therapeutic chemical space.

03
AI-Driven Discovery

Ultra-large foundation models trained on high-fidelity biological data to systematically identify new classes of disease-modifying molecules.

The Integrated Platform

Three disciplines,
one coherent system

Each capability amplifies the others — creating a discovery flywheel that no single-discipline approach can replicate.

Optogenetics & Synthetic Biology
